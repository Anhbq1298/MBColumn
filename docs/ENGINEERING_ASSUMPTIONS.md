# Engineering Assumptions

- Rectangular section only.
- Section centroid is the internal coordinate origin.
- Axial compression is positive.
- Concrete tension is ignored.
- Concrete compression uses fiber integration at `0.85 fc`.
- Steel strain follows plane-section compatibility.
- Steel stress is `Es * strain`, capped at `+/- fy`.
- Moments are about the section centroid.
- Slenderness and second-order effects are not included.
- Results are for study and preliminary checking only.
